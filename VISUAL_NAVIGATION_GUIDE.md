## Complete Click Flow for Demo

```
START: http://localhost:3000/

  ↓ Click "Submit Property"
  
http://localhost:3000/submit (Fill form)

  ↓ Click "Analyze with AI"
  
http://localhost:3000/analysis/demo-1 (View results, risk gauge)

  ↓ Scroll down, see escrow section
  
(Review all fraud detection checks)

  ↓ Click "Dashboard" in header
  
http://localhost:3000/ (Back to home)

  ↓ Click "View Reputation Scores"
  
http://localhost:3000/reputation 

  ↓ Click wallet button in header
  
(Phantom modal appears)

  ↓ Connect wallet
  
http://localhost:3000/reputation (Now shows reputation dashboard!)

  ↓ Click "Start Your First Transaction"
  
http://localhost:3000/submit (Back to form to continue flow)

COMPLETE! ✅
```
